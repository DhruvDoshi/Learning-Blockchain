04. Do You Need a Blockchain?

Do you need a Blockchain?

In the last section, we discussed the differences between blockchains as a database and traditional databases.

When looking at these different databases, one thing that immediately comes to mind for me is, "When would I use one or the other?". That's a really important point to understand. Given the news and headlines about the blockchain it might seems like its the perfect fit for every situation. That's not necessarily true, and as a blockchain developer, it will really help you to understand when a blockchain is the right solution for your application.

Throughout this section, we'll discuss a few ideas to consider when you're trying to decide whether or not to use a blockchain.

Blockchain Questions Overview

In this first video, we'll discuss a few questions you can ask to determine if a blockchain is right for your application.

Do You Need A Blockchain

5 Questions from video

Here are 5 questions from the video above to help you determine if a blockchain is right for your situation. Remember, answering yes to any one of them doesn't necessarily mean a blockchain is the right solution, but if you can answer yes to ALL OF THEM then a blockchain might be appropriate for your application.

  • Do you need a database?
  • Does it require shared write access?
  • Will you need to create the trust between users?
  • Can you operate without trusted 3rd parties?
  • Can you operate without control over permissions?

Try using these questions to help you come up with solutions for the scenarios listed below.
