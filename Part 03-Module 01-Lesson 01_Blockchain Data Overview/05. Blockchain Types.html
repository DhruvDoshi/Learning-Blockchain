Blockchain Types

Once you've decided that a blockchain is the right solution, you'll need to pick a specific type of blockchain.

In this section, we'll go over the different blockchain types, then discuss a few questions you can use to decide which type is right for your application.

To start, the next video discusses the different types of blockchains; public, private, and hybrid. We'll define their core ideas, then discuss important ways that they differ from one another.

3 Questions From the Video Above

Here are 3 questions from the video above to help you determine which type of blockchain is right for your situation:

  • Will transactions be public?
  • Will other companies need access to your data?
  • Should some information be public while other information is restricted?
