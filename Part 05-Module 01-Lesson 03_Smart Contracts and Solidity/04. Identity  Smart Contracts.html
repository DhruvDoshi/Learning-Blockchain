04. Identity & Smart Contracts

## Identity & Smart Contracts

You already know the importance of identity to interact with the blockchain. Here, we will explore the Ethereum public address which allows you to have an identity on the Ethereum network. It allows for smart contracts and other users to allocate resources or assets to your wallet.

To help us visualize the Ethereum identity, we will be using CryptoPunks, a crypto-arts project that allows us to visualize proof of ownership of collectibles stored on the Ethereum blockchain.

The owner for this crypto punk is identified by their Ethereum public wallet.
